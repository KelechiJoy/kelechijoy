WHO ARE WE?

Mountain of Fire and Miracles Ministries (MFM) is a full gospel ministry devoted to the Revival of Apostolic Signs of Holy Ghost fireworks and the unlimited demonstration of the power of God to deliver to the uttermost; absolute holiness within and without as the greatest spiritual insecticide, and the prerequisite for Heaven is taught openly. MFM is a do-it-yourself Gospel ministry where your hands are trained to wage war and your fingers to do battle.
At MFM, aggressive prayer is considered as an aid to spiritual focus and a check against being overwhelmed by the flesh.
